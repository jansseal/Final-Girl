In Neural Eclipse, you'll embark on an immersive journey through a dystopian world fraught with peril and intrigue. As you navigate the intricate narrative, you'll encounter pivotal moments where your choices shape the course of the story. From deciding the fate of key characters to unraveling the mysteries of a fractured society, every decision holds consequences that ripple throughout the game. Use your wits and intuition to forge alliances, confront adversaries, and ultimately, determine the destiny of the world. Are you ready to embark on this epic adventure?

WARNING: All choices are final and backtracking is not allowed.
